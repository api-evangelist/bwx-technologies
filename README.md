# BWX Technologies (bwx-technologies)
BWX Technologies is a manufacturer of nuclear components and provider of nuclear services to the U.S. government and commercial customers worldwide. The company delivers innovative nuclear solutions across naval propulsion, small modular reactors, nuclear fuel, medical isotopes, and complex site operations.
